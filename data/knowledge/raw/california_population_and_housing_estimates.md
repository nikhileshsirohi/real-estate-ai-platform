# California Population and Housing Estimates

## Summary
This document summarizes official California state demographic estimate sources that can help explain housing demand pressure, population shifts, and broad supply context.

## Key Facts
- The California Department of Finance publishes January population and housing estimates for cities, counties, and the state.
- The Department of Finance provides E-1 annual population and housing estimates with annual percent change.
- The Department of Finance also provides E-5 population and housing estimates for cities, counties, and the state across recent years.
- These sources are useful for tracking whether local housing demand may be supported by population and housing-stock changes over time.

## Advisory Use
- Use this document when an answer needs official California estimates rather than model-internal feature importance alone.
- These estimates are useful for contextual explanations about demand, supply pressure, and demographic trend direction.
