# California Housing Snapshot

## Summary
This starter knowledge document captures official California housing context that can support retrieval-augmented answers about the statewide market environment.

## Key Facts
- California's population estimate was 39,355,309 as of July 1, 2025.
- California had 14,877,904 housing units as of July 1, 2024.
- The owner-occupied housing unit rate was 55.9% in 2020-2024.
- The median value of owner-occupied housing units was $734,700 in 2020-2024.
- Median selected monthly owner costs with a mortgage were $2,946 in 2020-2024.
- Median selected monthly owner costs without a mortgage were $829 in 2020-2024.
- Median gross rent was $2,036 in 2020-2024.
- Building permits totaled 101,545 in 2024.
- California averaged 2.84 persons per household in 2020-2024.

## Advisory Use
- Use this document when answering broad statewide affordability, ownership-cost, rent, or housing-supply questions.
- This is statewide context and should not be treated as a city-level comp set.
