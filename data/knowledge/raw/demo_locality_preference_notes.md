# Demo Locality Preference Notes

## Summary
This document provides demo locality-level notes based on the seeded listing inventory. These notes are intended to help the recommendation and advisory layers explain search tradeoffs more specifically than statewide California context.

## Locality Highlights
### Oakland
- **Lake Merritt**: better fit for buyers who want a balanced city lifestyle with commute access and urban amenities.
- **Temescal**: stronger fit for buyers who want a neighborhood retail feel and townhouse-style living.
- **Rockridge**: better fit for higher-budget family or owner-occupier searches, especially when BART access matters.
- **Uptown**: better for lower-entry city buyers than large-family buyers.
- **Jack London Square**: better for transit-connected urban buyers than for family-size space seekers.
- **Montclair**: better for larger-home and family-oriented searches than for budget-constrained buyers.

### San Jose
- **North San Jose**: better for commuter-oriented or employer-adjacent searches.
- **Downtown San Jose**: stronger for urban, entry-level, or transit-friendly searches.
- **Willow Glen**: balanced owner-occupier location for townhouse buyers.
- **Almaden Valley** and **Evergreen**: stronger fit for larger-family space than for budget or starter searches.

### San Francisco
- **SoMa** and **Mission District**: stronger fit for urban, commute, or investment-oriented searches.
- **Sunset District**, **Inner Sunset**, and **Noe Valley**: better fit for buyers who prioritize neighborhood livability over lowest entry price.
- **Richmond District**: more suitable for starter condo positioning than premium family-house searches.

### San Diego
- **Mission Valley** and **Hillcrest**: stronger fit for transit, central access, or smaller-unit searches.
- **North Park** and **Clairemont**: more balanced for owner-occupier and family-style searches.
- **La Jolla**: premium lifestyle positioning with less budget flexibility.
- **Little Italy**: better fit for urban/investment-style searches than larger-family needs.

### Sacramento
- **Midtown** and **Downtown**: stronger for lower-entry urban searches.
- **Natomas**: practical value-oriented townhouse option.
- **East Sacramento**, **Land Park**, and **Curtis Park**: stronger fit for owner-occupier and family-style searches.

## Advisory Use
- Use this document when explaining why one returned listing may fit a lifestyle or search intent better than another.
- These are demo preference notes derived from the seed inventory and listing descriptions, not external market research.
