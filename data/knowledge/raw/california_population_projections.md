# California Population Projections

## Summary
This document summarizes official California population projection resources that can support long-range housing demand commentary.

## Key Facts
- The California Department of Finance Demographic Research Unit publishes state and county population projections through 2070.
- The published baseline includes total population projections, age breakdowns, sex breakdowns, and county projections.
- These projections are useful for long-horizon advisory responses, such as where sustained demand may matter more than a single-year price estimate.

## Advisory Use
- Use this document when questions ask about future housing pressure, long-term demand, or population-driven planning context.
- Projection outputs should be treated as planning context, not direct transaction-price evidence.
